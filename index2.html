Class Rules

  1. It's mandatory to attend classes, recordings are for revision not for first-time learning
  2. Do join live classes from laptop/desktop, not from mobile.
  3. Starting 10min and ending 10min of every class is for doubt session.
  4. Feel free to ask tough questions to your instructor. In case you asking questions in Live classes then either you are a champ or you aren't really learning.
  5. Make sure you practice class questions after every class, watching video aren't going to help you to crack interviews.
  6. Make sure you complete class assignments max by the end of the current week in which the class was conducted.
  7. Do share your assignment on the channel and seek feedback from your instructor and peers
  8. Collaborate with each other by making friends/group/team in order to learn together and grow together at a faster pace.
